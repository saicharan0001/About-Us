We the Hostel Website Committee designed a website to make your work easier when it comes to registering for hostel and mess by digitalizing the process. Our mission is to provide user friendly services in addition to managing the online registration process, we also work closely with the hostel and mess management to ensure that all facilities are well-maintained. Using website, student can know the menu of each mess also they can get mess card and pay dues along with extras he/she had. They can rate the mess as per their experience with the mess they took. Hostel and mess staff can take ride of all the details of the students and Hostel office admin can notify the user with updates.
Our committee is comprised of a dedicated team of individual who work tirelessly to provide user friendly and efficient service to all of you. We have an eminent committee of esteemed faculties. Chairman of our committee, Dr. Chandra Shekar Bestha (Warden MHB I). Dr. Raju Hazari (Warden E Hostel) and Dr. S Sheerazuddin (Warden MHB I) as the key members of the committee.
